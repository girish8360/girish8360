    Riders & Add-ons

    As of 2025, Niva Bupa (formerly Max Bupa) has introduced several popular riders and add-ons that customers can opt for to enhance their base health insurance coverage:

    ReAssure Rider (Available with some plans)

    Offers unlimited reinstatement of the sum insured for unrelated and related illnesses in a year. Once the base Sum Insured is used up, the rider activates automatically without any extra premium during the claim.
    Safeguard+ Very popular add-on, especially with ReAssure 2.0 and Health Companion plans.
    Key Benefits:
  • Coverage for non-payable expenses (e.g., consumables like gloves, PPE kits, masks).
  • No Impact on No Claim Bonus (NCB) even if a small claim is made.
  • Inflation Protection: Sum insured automatically increases each year in line with inflation (up to 10%).
    •
    Hospital Cash Provides daily cash benefit for each day of hospitalization (above 24 hours). Useful for covering miscellaneous expenses like travel, food, attendant charges that are not covered by base policy.
    Personal Accident Cover Add-on Covers accidental death and permanent total disability. Lump sum payout to the nominee or the insured, depending on the incident.
    Critical Illness Rider Covers a list of major critical illnesses like cancer, heart attack, kidney failure, etc. Provides a lump sum payout upon diagnosis, which can be used for treatment or lifestyle adjustment.
    OPD & Wellness Add-on (select plans) Coverage for Outpatient expenses like doctor consultation fees, pharmacy bills, diagnostic tests, etc. Also includes wellness programs, health check-ups, and discounts.
    Room Upgrade Rider Allows choosing any room category without worrying about sub-limits, depending on the plan. Especially useful for high-end hospitalizations where you prefer a single room or suite.
