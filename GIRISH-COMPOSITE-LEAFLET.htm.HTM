Multiple Plan Variants: STPP offers 7 distinct plan variants, allowing policyholders to choose coverage that aligns with their specific requirements:
  • Regular Cover: Provides a fixed sum assured throughout the policy term.​
  • Rebalancing Cover: Initially allocates 10% of the sum assured to life cover and 90% to accidental death benefit, with annual adjustments until the entire sum assured becomes life cover.​.​

  • Whole Life Cover: Provides coverage up to 100 years of age, with partial premium returns at specified milestones.​
  • Income Protection Cover: Delivers monthly income to beneficiaries in the event of the policyholder's death, with options for level or inflation-adjusted payouts
  • Special Exit Value (SEV)Policyholders have the option to exit the policy from the 30th policy year onwards (excluding the last 4 policy years) and receive up to 200% of total premiums paid, depending on the policy term and premium payment term.

  • Cover Continuance Benefit Allows deferral of premium payments for up to 12 months after the due date, while maintaining full risk cover, provided the policy has been in force for at least 3 years with all premiums paid
  • Terminal Illness Benefit In the event of a terminal illness diagnosis, an accelerated payout of up to ₹1 crore is provided, depending on the plan variant
  • Insta Claim Payment Offers expedited claim processing with partial payouts within one working day of claim intimation, subject to submission of mandatory documents

    SMART WEALTH ADVANTAGE GUARANTEE (SWAG) PLANS

    The Axis Max Life Smart Wealth Advantage Guarantee (SWAG) Plan is a comprehensive financial solution designed to cater to the sophisticated needs. It seamlessly integrates guaranteed returns with lifelong protection, facilitating effective legacy planning.
    The Smart Wealth Advantage Guarantee (SWAG) Plan offers a suite of five distinct variants, each tailored to meet specific financial goals and life stages.

    1. Wealth for Milestones (Variant 1)

  • Objective: Ideal for individuals seeking a lump-sum payout at maturity to fund significant life goals
    such as purchasing a home or financing higher education.
  • Premium Payment Terms: Single Pay or Regular Pay options of 5, 6, 8, 10, or 12 year
    1. Maturity Benefit: Sum Assured on Maturity plus Accrued Guaranteed Additions.
    2. Survival Benefit: Not applicable.
    3. Death Benefit: Sum Assured on Death or 105% of Total Premiums Paid, whichever is higher
    4. Policy Continuance Benefit (PCB): Optional.
  • Ideal For: Individuals aiming for a guaranteed lump-sum corpus at a specific future date
    •

    2. Regular Wealth (Variant 2)

  • Objective: Designed for those desiring a steady income stream post the premium payment term
  • Premium Payment Terms: Options of 5, 6, 8, 10, or 12 years.
    1. Survival Benefit: Regular Income Benefit plus Loyalty Income Booster (20% of the Income Benefit).
    2. Maturity Benefit: Not applicable
    3. Death Benefit: Sum Assured on Death or 105% of Total Premiums Paid, whichever is higher
    4. Policy Continuance Benefit (PCB): Optional.
  • Ideal for: Those desiring a regular income stream for a specified period.
    •

    3. Long-Term Wealth (Variant 3)

  • Objective: Caters to those looking for extended income periods along with return of premiums (ROP)at maturity.
  • Premium Payment Terms: Options of 5, 6, 8, 10, or 12 years.
    1. Survival Benefit: Regular Income Benefit plus Loyalty Income Booster (40% of the Income Benefit).
    2. Maturity Benefit: Return of Total Premiums Paid.(ROP)
    3. Death Benefit: Sum Assured on Death or 105% of Total Premiums Paid, whichever is higher
    4. Policy Continuance Benefit (PCB): Optional.
  • Ideal for: Those seeking income starting early in the policy term.
    •

    4. Early Wealth (Variant 4)

  • Objective: Suited for those requiring income to commence early, starting from the first policy year or month.
  • Premium Payment Terms: Options of 5, 6, 7, 8, 10, 12, 15, or 20 years.
    1. Survival Benefit: Regular Income Benefit starting as early as the first policy month.
    2. Maturity Benefit: Sum Assured on Maturity plus Accrued Guaranteed Additions.
    3. Death Benefit: Sum Assured on Death or 105% of Total Premiums Paid, whichever is higher
    4. Policy Continuance Benefit (PCB): Not Applicable.
  • Ideal For: Individuals seeking immediate income post policy commencement.
    •

    5. Lifelong Wealth (Variant 5)

  • Objective: Tailored for those aiming for lifelong income support, especially during retirement years.
  • Premium Payment Terms: Options of 5, 6, 8, 10, or 12 years
    1. Survival Benefit:
      1. Regular Income Benefit until the age of 100.
      2. Loyalty Income Booster (40% of the Income Benefit).
      3. Money Back Benefit: 50% of the Sum Assured on Maturity at age 85.
    2. Maturity Benefit: 100% Return of Total Premiums Paid at age 100.
    3. Death Benefit: Sum Assured on Death or 105% of Total Premiums Paid, whichever is higher
    4. Policy Continuance Benefit (PCB): Not Applicable.
  • Ideal for: Individuals desiring lifelong income, especially post-retirement.
    •
